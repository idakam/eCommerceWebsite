About Us

Extraordinary Stitching is a Austin based sister operating, specializing in handmade and vintage items. All items are made or curated by Audrey and Emma H.

We create handmade bags, potholders, general household items in a variety of colors, fabrics and mediums. We are also commission based, so please look through our site and see if there is anything that we are missing and we would be happy to make it for you. Please visit our contact page for information on how to message us.
